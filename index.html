Services & Custom Work

Every piece is made to order. Pricing is based on design complexity, materials, and timeline. Below are general guidelines—final quotes are provided after consultation.

Formal Gowns

Custom gowns designed and fitted for your body and event. Most gowns require around 40 or more hours of labor plus all fabric and supply costs. Because each design is unique, gown pricing is quote-based. Message for a personalized quote based on your design, fabrics, and timeline.

Cosplay Builds

Character-inspired looks, armor-style builds, and performance-ready cosplay. Cosplay is priced similarly to formal gowns—many projects require 40+ hours of labor plus materials, and are quoted individually after discussing the character, details, and deadline.

Performance Wear

Custom costumes and stage pieces for dancers, performers, and entertainers. Standard projects are billed at $35/hour for labor, plus materials. Rush projects follow the same rush structure as other custom builds, with higher hourly rates if your deadline is under a month.

Lingerie & Corsetry

Custom-fit corsets, bodysuits, and lingerie sets built for support, comfort, and confidence. Standard labor is billed at $40/hour, plus materials. Rush projects use the same rush rates as other custom work when deadlines are tight.

Alterations

Select alterations on formalwear and special pieces to improve fit and comfort. Standard alteration work is billed at $30/hour for labor. Rush alterations are billed at a higher hourly rate when you need them completed quickly.

For all projects, final pricing is confirmed after a short consultation about design, fabric choices, timeline, and fittings. Rush projects may be billed at higher hourly rates depending on your deadline.

FAQ & Policies

A quick look at how custom work is booked, priced, and scheduled with Wickedly Stitched by Kate.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. A 50% deposit is required to reserve your spot on the schedule and begin work on your project. The remaining balance is due at or before pickup/final delivery.

How do rush orders work?

If you need your piece on a tight timeline, rush pricing applies to labor so that late requests are scheduled fairly and sustainably:

  • For all custom builds (formal gowns, cosplay, performance wear, lingerie and corsetry):
    • Deadlines under one month are billed at $60/hour for labor.
    • Deadlines under two weeks are billed at $80/hour for labor.
  • For alterations:
    • Standard alteration work is $30/hour.
    • Rush alterations with deadlines under one month are billed at $50/hour.
    • Rush alterations with deadlines under two weeks are billed at $70/hour.

Rush availability depends on Kate’s current workload. Materials and supplies are always charged separately from labor.

How long does a formal gown take?

Most custom gowns require at least 40 hours of labor plus time for sourcing materials and fittings. It’s best to reach out as early as possible, especially for weddings, formals, or major events.

Where are fittings done? Do you ship?

Fittings are primarily available in the Franklin County, VA area. Some items—such as lingerie, certain cosplay elements, and select performance pieces—may be able to be shipped depending on the design and fit requirements. Full gowns and heavily structured garments typically require in-person fittings.

How do I get a quote?

Click Request a Piece to email directly about your project, or email katethestitchwitch@gmail.com . Share your idea, event date, and any inspiration photos. Kate will follow up with questions, a timeline estimate, and a custom quote.

About the Designer

The story and skill behind the magic ✨

Katelyn “Kate the Stitch Witch” Law Sarver is the owner and designer behind Wickedly Stitched by Kate. A Franklin County native with over a decade of sewing experience, Kate creates custom gowns, cosplay, performance wear, corsetry, lingerie, and alterations that celebrate every body and every story.

Her passion for design began young, inspired by the confidence a well-crafted garment can unlock. After caring for family and honoring a promise to her late mother—her greatest supporter—Kate turned her dream into reality: a studio where fashion is stitched with a little magic.

As an award-winning cosplayer and professional seamstress with experience in both custom work and industrial production, Kate blends technical skill with bold creativity. Whether you’re stepping onto the stage, walking down the aisle, or transforming into a character you love—Kate ensures you feel powerful, seen, and spectacular.

About the Designer

The story and skill behind the magic ✨

Katelyn “Kate the Stitch Witch” Law Sarver is the owner and designer behind Wickedly Stitched by Kate. A Franklin County native with over a decade of sewing experience, Kate creates custom gowns, cosplay, performance wear, corsetry, lingerie, and alterations that celebrate every body and every story.

Her passion for design began young, inspired by the confidence a well-crafted garment can unlock. After caring for family and honoring a promise to her late mother—her greatest supporter—Kate turned her dream into reality: a studio where fashion is stitched with a little magic.

As an award-winning cosplayer and professional seamstress with experience in both custom work and industrial production, Kate blends technical skill with bold creativity. Whether you’re stepping onto the stage, walking down the aisle, or transforming into a character you love—Kate ensures you feel powerful, seen, and spectacular.

FAQ & Policies

Quick answers — clean and easy to scan.

Booking Do you require a deposit?
⌄
Yes. A 50% deposit is required to reserve your spot on the schedule and begin work. The remaining balance is due at or before pickup/final delivery.
Rush timelines How do rush orders work?
⌄
For custom builds (formal gowns, cosplay, performance wear, lingerie/corsetry):
  • Deadlines under one month: $60/hour labor
  • Deadlines under two weeks: $80/hour labor
For alterations:
  • Standard: $30/hour
  • Under one month: $50/hour
  • Under two weeks: $70/hour
Rush availability depends on the current schedule. Materials are separate from labor.
Timing How long does a formal gown take?
⌄
Most custom gowns require at least 40 hours of labor plus sourcing time and fittings. Reach out as early as possible for weddings, formals, or major events.
Fittings Where are fittings done? Do you ship?
⌄
Fittings are primarily available in the Franklin County, VA area. Some items may be able to be shipped depending on design and fit requirements. Full gowns and heavily structured garments typically require in-person fittings.
Quotes How do I get a quote?
⌄
Click Request a Piece to email directly, or email katethestitchwitch@gmail.com . Share your idea, event date, and any inspiration photos. Kate will follow up with questions, a timeline estimate, and a custom quote.
